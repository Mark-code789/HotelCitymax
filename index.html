Comfort away from home

Hotel citymax is located at the heart of Nakuru County in between the city and the Nakuru National Park with plenty and varieties of rooms to choose from. We have deluxe, superior, tripple, twin and standard suites that are beautifully finished in a blend of comfort and convinience and furnished with an african theme and unique touch that turns a simple break into a treat.

Enjoy the beautiful view of the Nakuru city from the roof top, take pictures of the breathtaking sunrise and sunset views at your own comfort. Our conference halls with state of the art facilities; white boards, projector and ambient light provides you with exemplary experience perfect for your needs. Enjoy exquisite cuisines and fine delicacies from the restaurant at very affordable prices.
